Pizza

Pizza is a very popular and delicious food enjoyed by people all around the world. It is made with a soft or crispy base, topped with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and different toppings like chicken, vegetables, or pepperoni. When pizza is baked in the oven, the cheese melts and gives it a rich and tasty flavor. People often enjoy pizza with friends and family, especially at parties or gatherings.
